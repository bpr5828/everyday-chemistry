# Everyday Chemistry & Biotech Redesign Vision

This website is designed and developed by a High-School student who has a passion for the chemistry and biotechnology fields. It seeks to explore and provide resources to the public across two primary thematic pillars:

## Theme 1: Product & Consumer Chemistry Lab
- **Goal**: Educate consumers about the chemical components of everyday products (cosmetics, foods, household cleaners) and help them understand implications or reactions based on their specific health conditions or symptoms they might be unsure of.
- **Product Analyzer**: Users can paste an ingredient list and optionally input health conditions or symptoms. The tool provides a detailed breakdown of chemical components and displays specific "When to use" and "When NOT to use" recommendations based on the conditions provided.
- **Citizen Feedback & Social Learning**: Allow users to log real feedback and experiences regarding products or specific chemical compounds. This feedback is stored and shared to provide additional detail and warnings for other users.
- **Disclaimer Acknowledgment**: A mandatory disclaimer is displayed as an active pop-up modal upon visiting the site. It states that all information is for educational/information purposes only, is not medical advice, and users should consult a physician before acting on any suggestions.

## Theme 2: Clinical Trials & Regulatory Biotech
- **Goal**: De-jargonize the clinical drug development pipeline. Demystify how drugs are developed, how human clinical trials are designed (Phases I–IV), how efficacy is measured, and how regulatory agencies (like the FDA) review and approve therapeutics.
- **CDISC Data Validator**: Provide an interactive tool to validate simulated clinical trial datasets (human subjects) against industry standards like CDISC SDTM (Study Data Tabulation Model), which the FDA accepts for review. It highlights errors, warnings, and conformance status to teach users how data quality is evaluated for regulatory approvals.
- **Biotech Resources**: Provide links to databases, registries, and learning paths for aspiring students and curious citizens.
